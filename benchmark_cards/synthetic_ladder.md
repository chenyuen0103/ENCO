# Synthetic Ladder Card

## Intended Claims
- The framework can materialize new graphs from manifests rather than only consuming fixed BIF files.
- Synthetic scale and density can be controlled systematically for appendix studies.

## Unsupported Claims
- Synthetic results do not establish real-world validity.
- The ladder is not a replacement for real-graph benchmarks.

## Applicable Baselines
- `PC`, `GES`, `ENCO`

## Known Failure Modes
- Synthetic graphs can overestimate performance relative to real named graphs.
- Poorly chosen densities may trivialize or destabilize the task.
