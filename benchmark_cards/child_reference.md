# Child Reference Card

## Intended Claims
- Mid-size real graph for testing whether conclusions from Sachs generalize to a larger structure.
- Useful for ranking-sensitivity and representation-comparison analysis.

## Unsupported Claims
- Not a claim about pediatric decision support.
- Not sufficient on its own for scale arguments.

## Applicable Baselines
- `PC`, `GES`, `ENCO`

## Known Failure Modes
- Prompt length grows quickly under matrix formatting.
- Model variance is larger than on very small graphs.
